Common Questions

FAQ

What areas do you serve? ▾
We primarily serve Shelby Township, Troy, Utica, Sterling Heights, Warren, Clinton Township, Macomb, and surrounding communities in Macomb County. Not sure if we cover your street? Fill out the form and we'll confirm within 24 hours.
How do I get a quote? ▾
Just fill out the form below with your address and what services you need. We'll get back to you within 24 hours with pricing. No obligations.
How often should I get my lawn mowed? ▾
Weekly mowing is what we recommend and what we specialize in. It keeps your grass at the healthiest height, prevents thatch buildup, and makes your yard look consistently great all season long.
Why is weekly mowing better for my lawn? ▾
Cutting more than a third of the grass blade at once stresses your lawn and weakens the roots. Weekly mowing keeps cuts small and consistent, which means thicker, greener, healthier grass all season.
What happens if it rains on my scheduled mow day? ▾
We'll reschedule for the next available dry day and let you know. We never mow in conditions that could damage your lawn.
Do I need to be home when you service my property? ▾
Nope! As long as we have access to your yard, we can take care of everything while you're at work or away. We'll send a text when we're done.
How does snow removal work? ▾
We monitor forecasts and head out after snowfall. For regular customers, we aim to have driveways and walkways cleared before 7am so you can get to work on time.
